Understanding Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart's structure and function. Common types include coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and valvular heart diseases. Risk factors for heart disease include age, family history, smoking, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, obesity, sedentary lifestyle, diabetes, and stress.

About Heart Disease

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide, with millions of people affected by it every year. Early detection and prevention of heart disease are crucial in reducing the risk of death. Cardio-Ally shows great potential in predicting the risk of heart disease by analyzing various medical and lifestyle factors.
